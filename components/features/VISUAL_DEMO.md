## Interactive Elements

### Hover Effects
- **Table rows**: Slight lift + background color change
- **Buttons**: Scale up (105%) + shadow increase
- **Pills**: Background brightens
- **Charts**: Tooltip appears

### Click/Tap Actions
- **Dropdown button**: Opens/closes neighborhood list
- **Neighborhood in list**: Toggles selection
- **Pill X button**: Removes from comparison
- **Share button**: Copies URL, shows checkmark
- **Export button**: Triggers download
- **Close button**: Closes modal (if in modal mode)

### Animations
- **Page load**: Fade in with stagger effect
- **Add neighborhood**: Scale pop-in
- **Remove neighborhood**: Scale out
- **Dropdown open**: Slide down from top
- **Modal open**: Scale up + fade in
- **Chart load**: Fade in from sides
